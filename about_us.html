What do we do?

IntelEx Tutoring opened recently in 2022, with a great team of 6 amazing tutors. We have already helped countless students succeed in a variety of different elementary, high-school, and university level courses.

Our goal is to make learning a fun and rewarding experience for all. It is very normal for students to struggle with the style of teaching used in school. With a 1 on 1 experience, tutors can find the best possible style of learning for any student.
